## Style Prompt

Create a quiet premium export-brand film for YUKIMICHI - SNOWPATH JAPAN. The visual tone should feel trustworthy, restrained, and Hokkaido-born: deep navy canvas, warm gold accents, soft washi-tinted text, documentary product/logistics imagery, and calm crossfades. Avoid loud promotional motion, pop typography, neon palettes, and generic tech styling.

## Colors

- Deep navy background: `#07111f`
- Mid navy depth: `#0d1c35`
- Washi text: `#f8f5ef`
- Muted washi secondary: `#d9d0bf`
- Refined gold accent: `#d4b86a`
- Benigara reserve accent: `#8b1e2f`

## Typography

- Primary display: embedded `Yukimichi Mincho` from Windows Yu Mincho, serif fallback
- Secondary support: `Yu Gothic`, `Hiragino Sans`, Arial, sans-serif
- Letter spacing: slightly open for captions and CTA; no negative tracking.

## Motion

- Calm scene pacing, 0.65-0.75 second crossfades, slow zoom/pan on still imagery.
- Captions fade in quietly and remain legible against a subtle lower dark gradient.
- Logo closing should feel solemn and centered, with no bounce or flashy reveal.

## What NOT to Do

- No pop fonts, heavy gothic headline treatment, neon colors, or high-energy cuts.
- No AI/DX motifs, tech HUDs, glitch effects, or animated confetti.
- No susukino clip, no 4th image, no unsupported media references.
- No claims that export, customs, tariffs, HS codes, or delivery acceptance are guaranteed.
